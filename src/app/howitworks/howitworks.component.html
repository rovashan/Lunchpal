How It Works

1. Pick A Plan

Choose one of the delicious meal plans that suit your lifestyle and pocket. Each plans runs as a weekly subscription service. You can pause, switch or cancel your subscription at any week.

2. Order

Each morning, Lunchpal will remind you to order your lunch. You choose one of the available options for the day as well as when you would like it delivered to you. There is also a selection of side items that you can choose from for the same amount, instead of your main meal.

3. Enjoy

That's it! Now you can focus on your work while your lunch is freshly prepared and delivered to you.
