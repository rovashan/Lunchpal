Frequently Asked Questions

How many meals can I choose from each day?

There are 3 main meals you can choose from each day, as well a selection of items from the Snack Bar.

 Are the meals frozen or prepared in advance?

No, all meals are freshly prepared on the day.

 Can I skip weeks?

Yes, just login and pause your subscription for as long as you like.

 What if I have special dietary requirements?

The Lifestyle plan caters for special dietary requirements including low-carb, keto and high-protein diets.

 When will I be charged?

Each week, you will be automatically charged at 10 AM on Monday. Lunchpal will send you a reminder before this so you can pause or cancel your subscription for the week.
